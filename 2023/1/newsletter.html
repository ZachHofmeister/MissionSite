US Air Force Academy | January 2023

Mass at SEEK in the St. Louis Dome

The Air Force men on their way to the morning men's talk on day 3 of SEEK

Happy New Year!

I hope that you all had a very Merry Christmas and that the Lord fills 2023 with blessings for you! It was great seeing some of you when I came back to California and Arizona for the break, but if we didn't get a chance to catch up I hope that we will this Summer!

I can't imagine a better way to start 2023 than by attending the SEEK23 conference with our cadets, my teammates, and my sister! Our five days in St. Louis, MO were packed with 17,000 college students, daily mass, prayer, adoring Jesus in the Eucharist, praise and worship, and brilliant talks that encouraged onward in our journey with our Lord. We could already tell that new seeds of faith had been planted in our hearts, which we hope will continue to grow in bible study and discipleship this semester.

Speaking of bible studies and discipleship, we are already off and running in both! I will be leading 4 bible studies again this semester, and I have 2 men in growing discipleship (learning to share the faith with their peers and lead their own bible studies).
